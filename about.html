Hoosh! was founded in 2019 with the goal of providing an advanced logistics system with the goal of connecting cannabis consumers and dispensaries together in order to facilitate the purchase of cannabis products.

Hoosh! strives to delivery the highest quality products at the fast speed possible. At every corner we ensure that not only will your purchase arrive safety, but we work around the clock to optimize routes and our logistics services.

High Quality Products

Hoosh! works directly with your local dispensaries to ensure that your products are the highest quality possible. All products undergo our Hoosh Verified Certification process, involving lab testing, reports, and reviews.

"High" Education

The Hoosh! philosophy is that all users should know what they are using. That's why Hoosh! aims to educate users on all of our products so there is no uncertainty with the effects that they may pose.
